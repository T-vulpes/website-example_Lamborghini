ABOUT US

Automobili Lamborghini Spa

  • Automobili Lamborghini, founded in 1963, is headquartered in Sant'Agata Bolognese, Northern Italy.

  • Lamborghini currently produces two super sports car models: the Aventador with a V12 engine launched in 2011, the Huracán with a V10 engine launched in 2014, and the Urus Super SUV with a double turbo V8 engine launched in 2017.

  • For over half a century, Automobili Lamborghini has created an uninterrupted series of dream cars, including the 350 GT, Miura, Espada, Countach, Diablo, and Murciélago, as well as limited editions such as Reventón, Sesto Elemento, Veneno, and Centenario. In 2019, with the launch of the Lamborghini Sián FKP 37, consisting of only 63 units, the company offers unique new hybrid technologies that include a supercapacitor for hybridization, new material technology, and unparalleled Lamborghini performance.

  • In 2021, Lamborghini dedicated itself to the Countach for its 50th anniversary. The iconic Countach LPI 800-4, a limited and futuristic version of the super sports car, features groundbreaking design and breaks a technological rule.
